# Knowledge Platform

## Purpose

The Knowledge Platform captures, organizes, governs, and reuses organizational intelligence.

Knowledge becomes a reusable enterprise asset rather than remaining trapped inside projects.

---

# Problem Statement

Organizations repeatedly lose:

- Architecture Decisions
- Lessons Learned
- Modernization Insights
- Incident Learnings
- Delivery Knowledge

when projects end or team members leave.

---

# Vision

Convert:

```text
Project Experience
```

into

```text
Organizational Intelligence
```
